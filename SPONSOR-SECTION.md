## 💛 Sponsor CoTrackPro

CoTrackPro is a child-centered, trauma-informed documentation and safety platform — built to increase access to safety for families, professionals, and communities. Your support keeps this work independent, open, and free for the people who need it most.

### How funds are used

| Area | What it covers |
|------|---------------|
| **Infrastructure** | Hosting, CI/CD, domains, AWS services |
| **Development** | New features, security patches, platform stability |
| **Access** | Keeping core tools free for families and advocates |
| **Open Source** | Maintaining the [Access To](https://github.com/cotrackpro) civic tech initiative |
