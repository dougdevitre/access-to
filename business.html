Access to Business

From idea to launch — AI-guided startup support for aspiring entrepreneurs, small business owners, and startup mentors.

The problem

Aspiring entrepreneurs in Missouri face a maze of confusing registration requirements, scattered funding information, and a lack of clear guidance on how to turn an idea into a viable business. The resources that do exist are spread across dozens of agencies, websites, and programs — each with its own eligibility rules, deadlines, and application processes.

For first-time founders without connections to mentors or advisors, the gap between having a great idea and actually launching a business can feel insurmountable. Without structured support, too many promising ventures stall before they ever get off the ground.

Our approach

Access to Business provides AI-powered startup support tools that guide entrepreneurs from initial idea through launch. Built on proven business frameworks and Missouri-specific regulatory knowledge, these tools help users build lean business plans, navigate state registration, identify funding sources, and validate their market — all through guided conversation.

Every tool is open source, designed for real-world use, and built to work within the Claude.ai platform — no coding required, no cost to the end user.

Key Capabilities

Business Plan Builder

Guided, step-by-step lean business plan creation that helps entrepreneurs define their value proposition, target market, revenue model, and go-to-market strategy in a structured format.

MO Registration Guide

Step-by-step navigation through Missouri business registration requirements, including entity selection, Secretary of State filings, EIN applications, and local permits and licenses.

Funding Finder

Identify startup funding sources including Missouri-specific grants, SBA loans, angel investor networks, and crowdfunding strategies tailored to your business type and stage.

Market Validation

Frameworks and tools to test your business idea before launch — including customer discovery guides, competitive analysis templates, and minimum viable product planning.

Legal Structure

Clear guidance on choosing the right business entity — LLC, S-Corp, sole proprietorship, or partnership — with Missouri-specific tax implications and liability considerations.

Mentorship Matching

Connect with Missouri entrepreneurship resources including SCORE mentors, Small Business Development Centers, university incubators, and local startup communities.

Who this is for

Aspiring Entrepreneurs

First-time founders with a business idea who need structured guidance to navigate the journey from concept to launch, including planning, registration, and funding.

Small Business Owners

Existing business owners looking to formalize operations, explore new funding opportunities, or pivot their business model with AI-assisted strategic planning tools.

Startup Mentors

SCORE volunteers, incubator advisors, and entrepreneurship coaches who want structured tools to guide their mentees through business planning and launch processes.

Economic Development Staff

City and county economic development professionals, chamber of commerce staff, and workforce development teams supporting local entrepreneurship and small business growth.

How to use these tools

These are Claude Skills — structured AI prompt systems that run inside Claude.ai. To get started:

  1. Clone the repository from GitHub
  2. Upload the SKILL.md file to a Claude Project as a project knowledge file
  3. Start a conversation — the skill will guide you through its capabilities

No coding required. These tools work entirely through conversation with Claude.
