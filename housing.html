Access to Housing

PropTech intelligence for real estate professionals, investors, and researchers — market analysis, climate risk scoring, and Fair Housing-safe tooling.

The problem

Real estate professionals face an overwhelming landscape of market data, regulatory requirements, and analytical complexity. Fair Housing compliance adds critical constraints that most technology tools ignore or handle poorly — creating legal risk and perpetuating disparities.

Climate risk is reshaping property values and insurance markets, yet most practitioners lack accessible tools to evaluate environmental exposure at the property and portfolio level. The tools that do exist are expensive, fragmented, and require technical expertise most agents don't have.

Our approach

Better Broker provides AI-powered market intelligence designed from the ground up with Fair Housing compliance guardrails. Every analytical module includes safeguards against steering, discriminatory recommendations, and protected-class bias — so professionals can work confidently within legal and ethical boundaries.

The tool covers 74 modules across 6 analytical pods — from comparative market analysis to climate risk scoring — all accessible through structured conversation with Claude. No spreadsheets, no coding, no expensive subscriptions.

Key Capabilities

Market Analysis

Comparative market analysis, pricing strategy, absorption rates, and neighborhood trends — powered by structured AI reasoning with Fair Housing guardrails.

Fair Housing Compliance

Built-in guardrails against steering, discriminatory language, and protected-class bias. Every recommendation is checked for compliance before delivery.

Climate Risk Scoring

Property-level and portfolio-level environmental exposure analysis — flood zones, wildfire risk, heat islands, and insurance market impacts.

Investment Evaluation

Cap rate analysis, cash-on-cash returns, rehab cost estimation, and rental market intelligence for investors evaluating acquisition opportunities.

Report Generation

How to use these tools

These are Claude Skills — structured AI prompt systems that run inside Claude.ai. To get started:

  1. Clone the repository from GitHub
  2. Upload the SKILL.md file to a Claude Project as a project knowledge file
  3. Start a conversation — the skill will guide you through its capabilities

No coding required. These tools work entirely through conversation with Claude.
