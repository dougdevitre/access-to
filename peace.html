Access to Peace

De-escalation tools, trauma-informed communication, safety planning, and child-centered messaging — helping families move from conflict toward stability.

The problem

High-conflict family situations — custody disputes, protection order cases, and co-parenting breakdowns — often escalate because parents lack structured tools for de-escalation and child-centered communication. Without intervention, children become collateral damage in adult conflict.

Professionals working with these families (therapists, mediators, attorneys, and advocates) need practical, evidence-based frameworks that can be deployed quickly in crisis moments and sustained over time. Existing resources are either too clinical to be actionable or too simplistic to address real complexity.

Our approach

This pillar extends CoTrackPro's plugin architecture with specialized skills for de-escalation, safety planning, and trauma-informed messaging. Each tool is built around child-centered principles — keeping children's wellbeing at the center of every interaction.

Lois247 provides 24/7 AI-powered support for navigating difficult communication moments, while CoTrackPro Skills offers modular plugins for specific conflict scenarios. Together they create a comprehensive safety and communication toolkit for families in crisis.

Key Capabilities

Safety Planning

Structured safety plan templates that help families identify risks, establish boundaries, and create actionable steps for protection during high-conflict situations.

De-escalation Scripts

Ready-to-use communication templates for tense moments — reframing hostile messages, setting boundaries, and redirecting conversations toward child-centered outcomes.

Trauma-Informed Messaging

Communication frameworks that acknowledge trauma history, avoid re-traumatization triggers, and prioritize emotional safety for all family members.

Child-Centered Language

Age-appropriate messaging guides that help parents communicate about difficult topics — transitions, schedule changes, and conflict — without putting children in the middle.

Real-Time Guidance

Lois247 provides instant, 24/7 AI-powered support for navigating difficult moments as they happen — not hours later when the damage is done.

Plugin Architecture

CoTrackPro Skills provides modular, extensible plugins for specific conflict scenarios — add only the capabilities you need for your practice or situation.

Who this is for

Parents in High-Conflict Situations

Co-parents navigating custody disputes, protection orders, or communication breakdowns who need structured tools to keep interactions safe and child-focused.

Therapists & Counselors

Mental health professionals working with families in crisis who need evidence-based de-escalation frameworks and trauma-informed communication resources.

Mediators & Family Law Attorneys

Legal professionals who need practical tools for guiding clients through conflict resolution while maintaining child-centered standards.

Advocates & Social Workers

Domestic violence advocates, guardian ad litems, and social workers supporting families through safety planning and crisis intervention.

How to use these tools

These are Claude Skills — structured AI prompt systems that run inside Claude.ai. To get started:

  1. Clone the repository from GitHub
  2. Upload the SKILL.md file to a Claude Project as a project knowledge file
  3. Start a conversation — the skill will guide you through its capabilities

No coding required. These tools work entirely through conversation with Claude.
