Access to Safety

Safety planning tools, risk assessment frameworks, protective order navigation, and crisis resources — empowering families, advocates, and professionals to protect those at risk.

The problem

Families facing domestic violence, stalking, and high-conflict custody situations often lack structured, accessible tools for safety planning. Existing resources are fragmented across agencies, difficult to find in crisis moments, and rarely designed for the people who need them most.

Professionals — advocates, attorneys, social workers, and law enforcement — need practical frameworks they can deploy quickly and customize to individual circumstances. The gap between knowing safety planning matters and having actionable tools to do it remains dangerously wide.

Our approach

Access to Safety provides AI-powered safety planning tools that help individuals and professionals create comprehensive, personalized safety plans. Built on evidence-based frameworks and designed for real-world use, these tools guide users through risk assessment, safety strategy development, and resource connection.

Every tool is open source, trauma-informed, and built to work within the Claude.ai platform — no coding required, no cost to the end user.

For high-conflict co-parenting de-escalation and child-centered communication tools, see Access to Services.

Key Capabilities

Safety Planning

Structured, personalized safety plan templates that help individuals and families identify risks, establish boundaries, and create actionable steps for protection.

Risk Assessment

Evidence-based risk assessment frameworks that help professionals evaluate danger levels and prioritize interventions for individuals and families in crisis.

Protective Order Guidance

Step-by-step navigation for understanding and pursuing protective orders, including documentation preparation and court process orientation.

Crisis Resources

Curated connections to hotlines, shelters, legal aid, and community resources — organized by location and situation type for rapid access when it matters most.

Advocate Toolkit

Professional-grade tools for domestic violence advocates, social workers, and attorneys to support clients through safety planning and crisis intervention workflows.

Documentation Support

Guided documentation tools for recording incidents, preserving evidence, and building case files — structured for legal proceedings and agency reporting.

Who this is for

Individuals & Families at Risk

People experiencing domestic violence, stalking, or harassment who need structured guidance to create safety plans and connect with protective resources.

Domestic Violence Advocates

Shelter staff, hotline workers, and community advocates who support survivors through safety planning, resource navigation, and crisis intervention.

Family Law Attorneys & Legal Aid

Legal professionals navigating protective orders, custody safety concerns, and documentation requirements for clients in dangerous situations.

Social Workers & Therapists

Mental health and social service professionals who need evidence-based safety assessment tools and structured intervention frameworks for at-risk clients.

How to use these tools

These are Claude Skills — structured AI prompt systems that run inside Claude.ai. To get started:

  1. Clone the repository from GitHub
  2. Upload the SKILL.md file to a Claude Project as a project knowledge file
  3. Start a conversation — the skill will guide you through its capabilities

No coding required. These tools work entirely through conversation with Claude.
